## Join the NMTE

The New Musical Theatre Exchange welcomes committed musical theatre writers in the Twin Cities — composers, lyricists, and librettists working on new musicals.

### What Members Get

- **Monthly workshop** — share your work and receive constructive feedback from fellow writers
- **Readings** — opportunities to have your work read by actors and heard by audiences
- **Fiscal agency** — as a 501(c) nonprofit, NMTE can serve as fiscal agent for grants and funding
- **Production support** — guidance and community support for self-producing or co-producing your work
- **Collaboration** — connect with other writers to find creative partners

### How to Join

We welcome visitors to our monthly meetings. Come check us out and see if it's a good fit. If you're interested in becoming a member, [contact us](/contact/) or join our [Facebook group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/311900578864001/).
