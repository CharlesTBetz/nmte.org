Our monthly **Rough Cuts** reading — NMTE members share works-in-progress for feedback and development. Rough Cuts is where new musicals take their first steps, with writers hearing their material read and sung by fellow members.

This month featured scenes, songs, and lyrics from several works in development, with approximately 50 people in attendance.

Rough Cuts meets on the **third Sunday of each month**. [Contact us]({{ site.baseurl }}/contact/) if you're interested in attending.
