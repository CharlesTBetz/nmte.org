# Get Involved with NMTE

Interested in joining the New Musical Theatre Exchange? We welcome composers, lyricists, and librettists who are committed to developing original musical theater.

## Membership Process

Our membership process is designed to help you get to know our community and for us to get to know your work:

### Step 1: Audit Two Meetings
Attend two of our monthly meetings as an observer. This gives you a chance to:
- Experience our workshop format
- Meet current members
- Understand our collaborative approach
- See the variety of work being developed

### Step 2: Present Your Work
At a third meeting, present a piece of your musical theater work:
- A scene with dramatic dialog, music and lyrics
- An overall outline for a story to be musicalized
- A song with dramatic context

### Step 3: Membership Vote
Following your presentation, current members vote on your acceptance to the group.

## What We Look For

**Our focus** is on developing musical theater for readings, workshops, and eventual staging. We're looking for artists who:

- Are interested in creating **original musical theater** — telling compelling stories with words and music
- Want to **workshop and develop** their material with other creators
- Are interested in **collaboration and feedback**
- Have goals of getting their work **produced and performed**
- Can commit to **regular participation** in our monthly meetings

If creating original musicals for performance is your artistic goal, we welcome you to connect with us.

*Note: you do NOT need to have staged anything or be at any particular point in your professional career! Interest and some evidence of commitment and skill, along with a collaborative attitude, is what we are seeking.*
