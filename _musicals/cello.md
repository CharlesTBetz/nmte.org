A musical about the relationship between a musician and their instrument — the obsession, the joy, the frustration, and the moment you realize it might be time to stop. Written by NMTE member Ben Larson, *How to Play the Cello* was developed through NMTE workshops over several months and was selected for Theater Latté Da's NEXT Festival in 2025.
