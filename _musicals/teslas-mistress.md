A musical exploring the life and legacy of Nikola Tesla through a fictional relationship. Written by NMTE member Robert Elhai — a Tony and Drama Desk nominee for his orchestrations on Broadway's *The Lion King*. *Tesla's Mistress* had a staged reading at Unity South Twin Cities in October 2023.
