The story of Wilbur Foshay, the ambitious businessman who built Minneapolis's iconic Foshay Tower in the 1920s — and the spectacular rise and fall that followed. Written by NMTE member Kevin Bowen, *Foshay!* had its world premiere at Open Window Theatre in July 2023 and has been selected for the **Minnesota Fringe Festival 2026**.
