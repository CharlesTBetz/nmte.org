A woman's near-death experience shatters her marriage, her career, and everything she thought she knew about reality. *Five Minutes on the Low Road* explores what happens when someone returns from clinical death with absolute certainty that consciousness survives — and a world that isn't ready to hear it. Music by NMTE member Charles Betz, book by Josiah Thomas Turner. Selections were workshopped at Nautilus Music-Theater's Rough Cuts in March 2026.
