NMTE member **Kevin Bowen**'s musical *Foshay!* is now available to watch online. The musical premiered at Open Window Theatre in July 2023 with a 10-performance run.

*Foshay!* tells the story of Wilbur Foshay, the ambitious Minneapolis businessman whose iconic tower became a symbol of both aspiration and the excesses that led to the Great Depression. A collaboration between Kevin and **Lynn Bowen**, the show explores themes of ambition, legacy, and the cost of unchecked growth.
