The New Musical Theatre Exchange presented its **2026 New Works Cabaret** at <a href="https://www.croonersmn.com/" target="_blank">Crooners Supper Club</a> in Minneapolis — and sold out the house.

The evening took place in Crooners' intimate **Dunsmore Jazz Room**, where NMTE members shared original songs and scenes from works in progress with a packed audience of 87.

### Program

**"Strings Attached" and "Water from Snow"** from *Water from Snow* — Janet Preus, Fred Steele, and Robert Elhai; featuring Janely Rodriguez

**"Macak," "You Hold My Interest," and "How Do I Create?"** from *Tesla's Mistress* — Mariénne Kreitlow; featuring Tom Berg and Nicole Korbisch

**"With You"** from *Anzhelina & Karina* — Keith Benson; featuring Nicole Korbisch and Zakary Thomas Morton

**"How Could You Know?"** from *A House Divided* — Maureen Kane Berg, Tom Berg, and Mike Salmanson; featuring Carmelita Guse and Eryn Tvete

**"If I Only Knew"** from *Foshay!* — Kevin Bowen and Lynn Bowen; featuring Elena Glass

**"Up Against The Wall"** from *Metaland: A 21st C. Vaudeville* — Brenda Varda; featuring Elena Glass

**"Before The Baby Comes"** from *Socks & Shoes & Coats* — Rick Rhobajt Widen; featuring Anna Beth Baker

**"Time Travel" and "You Suck"** from *How to Play the Cello (Until You Don't)* — Benjamin Emory Larson; featuring Anna Beth Baker

**"Patches and Glue"** from *ACTS* — Rick Rhobajt Widen; featuring Elena Glass and Zakary Thomas Morton

**"It's Just the Rest of My Life!" and "Nowhere Particular"** from *It's Just the Rest of My Life!* — Mike Nilles; featuring Mark McCullough

**"The Symphony of You"** from *Metaland: A 21st C. Vaudeville* — Brenda Varda; featuring Zakary Thomas Morton

**"Somewhere Tonight"** from *A House Divided* — Maureen Kane Berg, Tom Berg, and Mike Salmanson; featuring Carmelita Guse and Eryn Tvete

**"Paint By Number World"** from *The Forger's Apprentice* — Kevin Bowen, Mark Forgy, and C.S. McNerlin; featuring Zakary Thomas Morton

**"With You (Reprise)" and "New Hope"** from *Anzhelina & Karina* — Keith Benson; featuring Nicole Korbisch and Zakary Thomas Morton

### Guest Vocalists
Elena Glass · Nicole Korbisch · Anna Beth Baker · Carmelita Guse · Eryn Tvete · Janely Rodriguez · Tom Berg · Zakary Thomas Morton · Mark McCullough
